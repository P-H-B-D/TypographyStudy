Typography Study 1:

Article & Layout

The following is a document that illustrates the first of my studies into web typography. This template is a proof of concept of a minimal sans-serif title element with serif body, fit for a blog, longform, or technical post. This template is inspired by "Professional Web Typography" by Donny Truong. This template may be reused free of charge with unlimited permissions, but without warranty.
